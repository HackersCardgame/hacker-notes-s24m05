A good "full description" should cover

* What you're talking about
* How you're going to go about it
* What your audience will learn

Third should answer the question "and what the fuck are your attendees going to get out of this?" (Or, "why should they attend?") An answer usually starts with "By the end of my talk attendees will X" -- learn, understand, comprehend, have tools for, etc.
