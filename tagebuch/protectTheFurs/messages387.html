Apps like this (TOTP and HOTP) are better than SMS, but they’re not truly a different kind of authentication than a password. The triad of authentication is:
• Something you know
• Something you have
• Something you are

TOTP and HOTP setup codes are could be construed as something you know, rather than something you have. A security key is a hardware device with a baked in cryptographic key, which is actually a physical thing that you have.
