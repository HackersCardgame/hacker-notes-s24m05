84 -85 – After I get my Mac, I start joining every local MUG, and I am calling Mac-oriented LA and Bay Area BBS’s a lot, and Seattle and Wash DC BBS’s occasionally. I am reading everything I can see that is helpful, and I am ‘cross-pollinating’ info between them.
Mid to Late 86 - I am visiting a friend in a University Computer Lab, and he is looking at some stuff on a mini-computer terminal. I quickly realize that he is getting this text from elsewhere.
