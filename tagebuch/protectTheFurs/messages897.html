Controversial opinion here ...

Back when I used to teach this sorta stuff, I used to tell my students I don't care how they learn the thing, I care that they learn the thing and can pass the tests.

So if they learn better by coming to hear me speak great. If they learn better by watching my recordings so they can fast forward and rewind and pause and all that, great, I don't want to see them in person because they're wasting their time.

If they learn better by watching someone else's Youtubes, great! I have all the topics I cover, if they can find someone else who covers those for them better than I can, all good. Come to my office hours to confirm that there aren't any gaps.

If they learn better reading someone else's text book, likewise great! Come to my office hours.

Where I'm going with this ... I think it's better to pick up the overall topic and knowledge however works best for you, even if it's not doing the course for the cert you want.
